Life Cycle of an Animal

  1. Birth
    • The animal is born or hatched from an egg.
  2. Growth
    • The animal grows in size and strength.
  3. Maturity
    • The animal becomes fully developed.
  4. Reproduction
    • The animal produces offspring.
  5. Aging
    • The animal grows old over time.
  6. Death
    • The life cycle comes to an end.
